About

My name is Joslynn Losee I am a 25 year old multidisciplinary artist who specializes in illustration and graphic design.

A bit about me

Just a local Salt Lake City artist
My creative career launched in 2019 with the opening of my Etsy shop.
I graduated MTECH in 2021 for graphic design
One of my very first listings went viral; launching me into the world of professional art.
Through trial and error, countless mistakes, and grit I have simplified my techniques and optimized my methods.
I am grateful for all of my friends and family that have supported me throughout the years.
I hope to continue to make works of art for all to enjoy!
